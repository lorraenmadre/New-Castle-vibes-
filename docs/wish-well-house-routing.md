# WISH WELL House Routing

New Castle uses the WISH WELL house system to classify documents, tasks, summaries, and outputs.

The goal is to make every document easier to understand, store, and act on.

## Routing Principle

Every document should receive:

- One primary WISH WELL house
- One optional secondary house
- One recommended storage location
- One recommended next action

The primary house tells the system where the document belongs.

The secondary house shows where it may also have impact.

## Document Examples

### Court or family documents

Possible routing:

- Primary: House 7
- Secondary: House 4 or House 1

Reason:

These documents often involve agreements, accountability, home structure, identity, or family business impact.

### Contracts and agreements

Possible routing:

- Primary: House 7
- Secondary: House 1, House 2, or House 5

Reason:

Contracts belong to the accountability house but may affect entities, money, or projects.

### Insurance documents

Possible routing:

- Primary: House 8
- Secondary: House 2 or House 4

Reason:

Insurance belongs to risk and protection, but may also involve assets or health/home matters.

### Trust documents

Possible routing:

- Primary: House 1 or House 9
- Secondary: House 2 or House 10

Reason:

Trusts may involve identity, governance, assets, education, or legacy.

### Business plans

Possible routing:

- Primary: House 5
- Secondary: House 2, House 3, or House 10

Reason:

Business plans are project documents, but they may involve money, tech, and public authority.

### Health or benefits records

Possible routing:

- Primary: House 4
- Secondary: House 8 or House 12

Reason:

These documents involve care, support, risk, and regulation.

### Research papers

Possible routing:

- Primary: House 9
- Secondary: House 6 or House 10

Reason:

Research belongs to education and higher learning, but may influence systems or public authority.

### Content and public assets

Possible routing:

- Primary: House 10
- Secondary: House 3 or House 11

Reason:

Public-facing material belongs to story and legacy, but may also involve communication or community.

## Routing Output Format

Each routed document should produce:

```text
Document:
Document Type:
Primary House:
Secondary House:
Reason for Routing:
Storage Destination:
Recommended Next Action:
Monday.com Task Needed:
Human Review Needed:
```

## Routing Rule

If a document is unclear, place it first in New Castle intake and assign a temporary review status.

Do not force unclear documents into the wrong house just to feel productive. That is how file systems become haunted swamps.

## Outcome

WISH WELL routing lets New Castle organize documents by meaning and function, not just file type.

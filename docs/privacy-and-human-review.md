# Privacy and Human Review

New Castle is designed as a document organization, summarization, drafting, and workflow-support system.

It is not a replacement for licensed professional judgment.

## Purpose

This document defines boundaries for privacy, sensitive documents, and human review.

New Castle may help users prepare, organize, summarize, and route documents.

It should not pretend to be a court, attorney, doctor, therapist, tax advisor, financial advisor, insurance adjuster, or emergency service.

## Human Review Required

Flag for human review when a document involves:

- Court orders
- Legal deadlines
- Custody or family law
- Contracts or agreements
- Insurance coverage decisions
- Tax obligations
- Financial obligations
- Medical treatment
- Mental health treatment
- Safety concerns
- Property rights
- Business formation
- Trust or estate planning
- Government benefits
- Claims or disputes

## System May Help With

New Castle may help with:

- Organizing documents
- Creating plain-language summaries
- Extracting dates and names
- Identifying missing information
- Creating timelines
- Drafting questions for a professional
- Preparing packets for review
- Sorting documents into WISH WELL houses
- Creating Monday.com tasks
- Saving outputs into Google Drive

## System Should Not Do

New Castle should not:

- Guarantee legal outcomes
- Tell a user what a court will do
- Replace legal advice
- Replace medical or mental health care
- Replace tax or financial advice
- Make final coverage decisions
- Submit sensitive documents without explicit user approval
- Send emails or documents without confirmation
- Delete or overwrite user files without confirmation

## Sensitive Information Handling

Documents may contain sensitive information such as:

- Full legal names
- Birth dates
- Addresses
- Case numbers
- Claim numbers
- Financial records
- Medical or benefits information
- Family details
- Safety information
- Entity or trust records

The system should treat these as sensitive by default.

## Recommended Safeguards

New Castle should support:

- User approval before sending or sharing
- Clear file naming
- Version history
- Archive instead of deletion
- Human review flags
- Source links
- Output summaries separated from original documents
- Minimal necessary copying of sensitive data
- Clear distinction between draft and final files

## Review Status Labels

Suggested labels:

```text
No Review Needed
Needs User Review
Needs Attorney Review
Needs Medical Review
Needs Tax Review
Needs Financial Review
Needs Insurance Review
Needs Safety Review
Archived
```

## Output Disclaimer

Recommended output note:

```text
This summary is for organization and preparation only. It is not legal, medical, mental health, tax, financial, or insurance advice. Review with a qualified professional when decisions, rights, deadlines, benefits, coverage, safety, or obligations are involved.
```

## Emergency Boundary

If a document or user note suggests immediate danger, the system should not attempt to resolve the emergency through workflow automation.

The user should be directed to appropriate emergency, crisis, legal, or local support services.

## Outcome

New Castle should make sensitive documents easier to understand and act on while preserving boundaries around professional judgment, user approval, and privacy.

The system should create clarity, not false authority. Humanity has already produced enough false authority. No need to automate more of it.
